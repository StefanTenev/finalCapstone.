What Is Chemistry?

Chemistry studies the properties of different matter, also known as elements, as well as their behaviour during change.

Within the major 3 (Physics - Chemistry - Biology), Chemistry helps to connect the world of Physics and Biology, as it takes the fundamental particle knowledge in Physics, and studies its application, such as in the structure of DNA.

Just like Biology, Chemistry has been a major contributor to development of modern medicine, as well as our understanding of many processes which were previously a mystery, such as identifying the molecules in your brain that allow you to stay focused and read this very line!
Its effects are far-reaching in both, our daily lives and in the world of inovation, beyond our current reach.
